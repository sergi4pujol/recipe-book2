# New Pasta Recipe

## Ingredients
- spaghetti
- cherry tomatoes
- garlic
- basil
- olive oil
- parmesan cheese

## Instructions
1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
3. Add halved cherry tomatoes to the pan and cook until they start to soften.
4. Toss in the cooked spaghetti and mix well to combine with the tomatoes and garlic.
5. Remove from heat and stir in fresh basil leaves.
6. Serve topped with grated parmesan cheese. Enjoy!
