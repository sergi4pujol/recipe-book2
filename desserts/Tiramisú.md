## Ingredients
250g mascarpone cheese good
3 big spoons of sugar
2 eggs
2 big spoons of sugar per egg (in the coffee)
Coffee
Cookies 

## Instructions
Whip the yolks and sugar together, add mascarpone cheese and then the egg whites, previously beaten with a pinch of salt. 
Dunk the cookies into the coffee, where you previously added with sugar. 
Start from adding in a tray a bit of the cream on the bottom and then the cookies, dunked in the coffee, then a layer of cream, and so on. 
Leave in the fridge for one night, before eating.
