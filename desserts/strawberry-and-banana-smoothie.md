# Strawberry and Banana Smoothie


## Preparation

- Find strawberries from freezer.
- Find blender.


## Ingredients

- 250 g frozen strawberries
- 0.5 dL water (if using frozen berries)
- 2 bananas
- 2.5 dL milk (high fat)
- 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla sugar


## Instructions

- Slice the banana into smaller pieces.
- Add all ingredients to blender and blend.
- Add more milk if too thick, add more milk.
